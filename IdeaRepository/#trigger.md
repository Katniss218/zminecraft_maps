
## Define `#trigger` as any of the following:

- Plain interactible:
  - Actuator (lever, button, pressure plate) 
  - Hidden actuator (trapped chest, tripwire, etc)
- Anything you can get a redstone output from
- Mob hurt
- Mob killed
- Player hurt
- Player killed
- Quest progressed
- Quest completed
- Quest failed
- Area
- Time
- Item in inventory
- Item in container
- Obscure multipart logical triggers
- etc.
